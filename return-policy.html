Return & Refund Policy

Understanding our refund and return procedures

Last updated: January 15, 2024 Policy Version: 1.3

Overview

At PixelVault, we strive to provide the best gaming experience possible. This Return & Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be granted for digital game purchases and physical merchandise.

Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase. By completing a purchase, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

Digital Game Refunds

Eligible for Refund

  • Technical issues preventing game launch or play
  • Incorrect game purchased (within 2 hours of purchase)
  • Game not as described or advertised
  • Duplicate purchases (accidental)
  • Server issues preventing access for 24+ hours

Not Eligible for Refund

  • Game has been played for more than 2 hours
  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Game performance on your hardware
  • Seasonal sales or price drops
  • Account bans or violations
  • DLC or expansion content

Refund Timeframes

Immediate Refunds

Within 24 hours of purchase, no questions asked (if game not launched)

Standard Refunds

Within 14 days of purchase, subject to eligibility criteria

Extended Refunds

Up to 30 days for technical issues or game-breaking bugs

Physical Merchandise Returns

For physical merchandise (gaming accessories, collectibles, etc.), the following conditions apply:

Return Conditions

  • Item must be in original condition and packaging
  • Return must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Proof of purchase required
  • Customer responsible for return shipping costs
  • Damaged or defective items will be replaced or refunded

Non-Returnable Items

  • Personalized or custom items
  • Downloadable content codes (once revealed)
  • Gift cards or store credits
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"

1

Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued using the original payment method:

Credit/Debit Cards

3-5 business days to appear on your statement

Digital Wallets

1-3 business days to return to your account

Store Credit

Immediate credit to your PixelVault account

Special Circumstances

Regional Variations

Refund policies may vary by region due to local consumer protection laws. Customers in the European Union are entitled to a 14-day cooling-off period for digital content.

Force Majeure

In cases of natural disasters, technical failures, or other extraordinary circumstances, refund processing may be delayed. We will communicate any such delays promptly.

Fraud Prevention

We reserve the right to investigate and deny refund requests that appear to be fraudulent or abusive. This includes excessive refund requests or attempts to circumvent our policies.

Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision, you may:

  1. Request a review by our senior support team
  2. Provide additional documentation or evidence
  3. Escalate to our customer relations department
  4. Contact relevant consumer protection agencies in your region
