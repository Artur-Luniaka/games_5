Delivery Issues

If you experience any delivery issues, we're here to help:

Lost Packages

If your package is lost in transit, we'll work with the carrier to locate it or provide a replacement/refund.

Damaged Items

If items arrive damaged, please document the damage with photos and contact us immediately for replacement.

Wrong Items

If you receive incorrect items, we'll arrange for the correct items to be sent and provide a return label.

Delivery Delays

For significant delays, we may offer partial refunds or expedited shipping on future orders.

International Shipping

For international orders, please note:

Customs & Duties

Import duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient. These charges are not included in our shipping costs.

Delivery Times

International delivery may take 7-21 business days depending on destination and customs processing.

Restricted Items

Some items may be restricted in certain countries. We'll notify you if your order contains restricted items.
